Davis' Chilli Con Carne

While Davis didn’t exactly spell out the ingredients or instructions for his beloved chili in his memoir, his first wife Frances, whom Davis trusted implicitly with the chili making, submitted the following to Best Life magazine in 2007. While you’re prepping, I recommend you put on 1956’s Cookin’ With the Miles Davis Quintet.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Melt suet in large heavy pot until liquid fat is about an inch high. Remove solid pieces of suet from pot and discard.

step 2

In same pot, sauté onion.

step 3

Combine meats in bowl; season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, chili powder, and cumin.

step 4

In another bowl, season kidney beans with salt and pepper.

step 5

Add meat to onions; sauté until brown.

step 6

Add kidney beans, consommé, and vinegar; simmer for about an hour, stirring occasionally.

step 7

Add more seasonings to taste, if desired.

step 8

Cook spaghetti according to package directions, and then divide among six plates.

step 9

Spoon meat mixture over each plate of spaghetti.

step 10

Top with Parmesan and serve oyster crackers on the side.

step 11

Open a Heineken.

ingredients

    ¼ lb. suet (beef fat)
    1 large onion
    1 lb. ground beef
    ½ lb. ground veal
    ½ lb. ground porksalt and pepper
    2 tsp. garlic powder
    1 tsp. chili powder
    1 tsp. cumin seed
    2 cans kidney beans, drained
    1 can beef consommé
    1 drop red wine vinegar
    3 lb. spaghetti
    parmesan cheese
    oyster crackers
    Heineken beer
