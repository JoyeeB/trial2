Amy's Pasta Ceci

"My sister Laura and her partner Tracey concocted this hot pasta recipe and gave it me in a cookbook, It has become a tradition in my house. Chickpeas, olive oil, onions, garlic, and Parmesan cheese—perfect combo and easy!" - Amy Ray.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

Heat olive oil in large skillet (with lid) on medium

step 2

Add chopped onions to sauté, stir frequently until onions are translucent and sweet.

step 3

Turn Heat to low, add garlic and stir (add more olive oil if it’s too dry at this point). Cook for about 5 more minutes.

step 4

Add chickpeas and enough cooking liquid until liquid is even with the surface of other ingredients in skillet. Simmer covered on low for 15 minutes.

step 5

To thicken sauce, use a fork to mash up some of the chickpeas. Add pepper and salt as needed.

step 5

Prepare pasta. Farfalle and Penne are great for this, but use what you want.

step 5

Serve chickpeas over warm pasta and most importantly—add plenty of shaved parmesan cheese.

ingredients

    3 16 oz. cans of chickpeas (or 2 cups dried chickpeas.)
    5 medium yellow onions (coarsely chopped)
    6 cloves of garlic (finely minced)
    2 tbs. of olive oil
    Freshly ground pepper
    Parmesan cheese (shaved)
