Beyoncé's Super Bowl Guacamole

Guacamole is especially popular on Super Bowl Sunday and Cinco de Mayo. Looking for a no-fail guacamole recipe for the game? Beyonce, who performed during the Super Bowl halftime, shared her favorite formula and all the ingredients are easy to find.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Peel avocados and remove the pit.

step 2

Smash with a spoon in a large bowl.

step 3

Add onion, garlic and tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper.

step 4

Mix it well.

step 5

Cover with plastic wrap and place in the refrigerator for about 20 minutes.

step 6

Fill corn chips scoops with guacamole and enjoy.

ingredients

    2 ripe avocados (a bit soft to the touch)
    1 small onion
    1 clove garlic (or a bit of crushed garlic)
    1 small tomato
    1 ½ Tablespoons lime juice
    Salt and pepper to taste
    Corn chip scoops
